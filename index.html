I hate the internet, I hate every stupid profile on every centralized social platform. Although I do use them, I don't feel right using them. Faceless profiles with a certain layout with you just having to type in your name, your bio and upload a picture tells me nothing about you. It is an utter polar opposite with personal sites.

My main interests are technology, music and just remembering the good ol' days. I am a big boomer, although I sometimes can try to hide it. I'm a big music man, if I'm not listening to something right now, my mind is already humming something from memory. I don't play any instruments, and I don't really have any favorite genres, the most I listen to is rock and synthwave/electronic.
